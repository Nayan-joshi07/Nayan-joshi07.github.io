About Me

From Software Engineer to
AI Engineering

I started my career building games at Zynga and Bombay Play, shipping features for millions of players using Unity, TypeScript, and C++. That foundation gave me a deep respect for production systems and user experience at scale.

Three years ago I made a deliberate shift into AI — joining Quilt Technologies to build LLM-powered content generation systems, then Seedling Labs where I now architect the full AI testing infrastructure behind Orchard: a VNC browser fleet on AWS ECS, an LLM prompt engineering layer for Playwright automation, and a production monitoring stack.

Along the way I conducted and fully remediated a VAPT covering 55+ security findings across backend, frontend, infrastructure, and cloud layers — something most AI engineers never touch.

Today I work at the intersection of LLMs, agentic systems, and DevOps. I'm available for consulting engagements that need someone who can go end-to-end — from architecture decisions to shipped code.
